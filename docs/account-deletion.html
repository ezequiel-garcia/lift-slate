Account Deletion

How to delete your LiftSlate account and data

Delete from the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the app:

  1. Open LiftSlate and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account".
  3. Type DELETE to confirm.
  4. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

Delete via Email

If you no longer have access to the app or cannot sign in, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to liftslate.support@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your LiftSlate account.
  4. We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (email, display name, preferences)
  • All exercise data and personal records (maxes)
  • All workout history
  • Gym memberships
  • Any content you created within gyms

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Gym Owners

If you are the owner of a gym, you must first transfer ownership or delete the gym before you can delete your account. This is to protect other gym members' access to shared workouts.

Contact

If you have questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at liftslate.support@gmail.com.
