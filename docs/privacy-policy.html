Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026

LiftSlate ("we", "our", or "the app") is operated by Ezequiel Garcia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the LiftSlate mobile application.

By using LiftSlate, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the app:

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Authentication credentials (securely managed by our authentication provider)

User-Generated Content

  • Exercise data and lift records (max weights, sets, reps, percentages)
  • Workout logs and history
  • Unit preferences and app settings
  • Gym information (name, description, logo image) if you create or join a gym
  • Gym membership and role information
  • Notes added to exercises or workouts

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App crash reports and error logs (via Sentry)
  • General app usage patterns for performance monitoring

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide the core app functionality (tracking lifts, calculating training weights, managing workouts)
  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Enable gym features (creating, joining, and managing gyms)
  • Monitor and fix app errors and crashes
  • Improve app performance and reliability

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase (database and authentication) — stores your account and app data on secure cloud servers hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the Asia Pacific (Mumbai) region. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Sentry (error tracking) — receives crash reports and error data to help us fix bugs. Does not receive your exercise data or personal content. Sentry Privacy Policy.
  • Google OAuth (optional sign-in) — if you choose to sign in with Google, Google processes your authentication. We only receive your email address and name. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Sign-In (optional sign-in) — if you choose to sign in with Apple, Apple processes your authentication. You may choose to hide your email. Apple Privacy Policy.

These services have their own privacy policies and handle data according to their respective terms.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS).
  • Your data is stored on Supabase-managed servers hosted on AWS infrastructure.
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using platform-native secure storage.
  • Database access is controlled through row-level security policies, ensuring users can only access their own data.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

Your data is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within your gym: If you join a gym, your display name and role are visible to other gym members. Coaches and admins in your gym may view workouts assigned to you.
  • Error reports: Anonymized crash and error data is sent to Sentry for debugging purposes.

We do not share your exercise data, lift records, or personal information with any other parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all your data is visible within the app.
  • Correct your data — you can update your profile, exercises, and maxes at any time.
  • Delete your data — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen in the app. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
  • Data portability — contact us to request a copy of your data.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen within the app. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile information is permanently removed.
  • All your exercise data, lift records, and workout history are permanently deleted.
  • Your gym memberships are removed.
  • This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

If you are unable to access the app, you may request account deletion by contacting us at liftslate.support@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

LiftSlate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. International Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with data protection laws:

  • Our legal basis for processing your data is the performance of our contract with you (providing the app service) and your consent.
  • You may exercise your rights under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR) by contacting us.
  • Your data is stored on servers in the Asia Pacific region (AWS Mumbai). By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
